About VirusLens

VirusLens aggregates results from multiple sources to give a quick, readable risk view for URLs and files.

Key Functions

  • Scan a single URL or upload a file for immediate risk verdict.
  • Bulk scanning: upload CSV or paste indicators of compromise (IOCs).
  • Human-friendly PDF reports (per scan) for sharing and submission.
  • History & Reports tracking across all your scans.

How it Works

  1. Go to Scan for quick checks (URL or file).
  2. Use Bulk for CSV uploads or pasted IOCs.
  3. Open Reports to generate a PDF for any completed scan.

API keys are read by the backend if configured.
